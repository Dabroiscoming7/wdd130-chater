INSTRUMENT FAMILIES

WOODWIND

Woodwind instruments produce sound by the vibration of air striking a reed or by air flowing across an opening.

BRASS

Brass instruments are characterized by three valves that, when pressed in various combinations, alter the pitch of the instrument.

PERCUSSION

Percussion instruments are struck by a person to produce sound, whether by hand or with an implement.

STRINGS

String instruments produce sound from the vibration of stretched strings when played by a bow, plucked, or struck.
